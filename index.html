The Problem We Solve

🔴 Enterprise AI Is A Black Box

Teams are spending hundreds of thousands on AI with zero visibility, control, or optimization

🔴 "Our AI bill doubled overnight"

Teams deploy OpenAI, Claude, Azure AI across departments. Finance gets shocked by £50K monthly bills with no breakdown of what's driving costs.

Real scenario: Marketing chatbot costs £15K/month, but they don't know it until the invoice arrives
Data: 73% of enterprises lack visibility into AI costs across teams (Deloitte State of AI 2024)

🔴 "We have no idea what teams are doing"

CIOs can't see which departments use which AI models, for what purposes, or whether they're getting value for money.

Real scenario: IT discovers 8 different AI tools being used without approval or oversight
Data: 68% of IT leaders report shadow AI adoption across departments (Gartner 2024)

🔴 "We're paying premium for everything"

Teams default to expensive models like GPT-4 for simple tasks that could run on cheaper alternatives, wasting 30-60% of AI spend.

Real scenario: Using £0.03/request GPT-4 for summaries that could cost £0.002 with GPT-3.5
Data: 30-40% of AI spend is wasted on inefficient optimization (McKinsey AI Report 2024)

🔴 "We can't govern what we can't see"

Compliance teams need to monitor AI usage for security, privacy, and regulatory requirements but have no centralized monitoring.

Real scenario: GDPR audit reveals customer data being processed by unmanaged AI APIs
Data: 81% of companies lack proper AI governance frameworks (PwC AI Governance Survey 2024)

🔴 "AI regulation is coming, and we're not ready"

With EU AI Act, US Executive Orders, and UK AI Safety Framework rolling out, enterprises need visibility, control, and auditability to demonstrate compliance. Without centralized AI governance, organizations risk regulatory penalties and reputational damage.

Real scenario: EU AI Act audit requires proof of AI usage tracking, risk assessment, and compliance controls - but there's no centralized system to provide audit trails
Data: 67% of enterprises are unprepared for AI regulation compliance (Gartner AI Risk & Governance Survey 2024)
